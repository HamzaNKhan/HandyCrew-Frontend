About us!

Handy Crew is a worker welfare system designed to transform how a person finds work. We bridge the gap between an employer and a worker by providing an easy to use online system for searching and hiring everyday workers such as drivers, maids, electricians, and plumbers, among a host of similar blue-collar / non-formal professions.
